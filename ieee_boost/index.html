About The Event

IEEE Boost is organized by the IEEE Sri Lanka Section Student Activities Committee (SAC) to provide a forum for student members to become aware of the substantial benefits that IEEE could have on their lives, as well as to better shape their lives in terms of leadership and career objectives.

F.A.Q

  • Who can attend?

    The IEEE Boost is an event where IEEE enthusiasts from all 21 Student Branches, of the IEEE Sri Lanka Section, gather to experience professional development, networking, knowledge-sharing sessions, and much more activities.

  • When & Where will be the event held?

    Due to the pandemic, the event will be held virtually via zoom on the 2nd and 3rd of July 2022 from 6 pm.

  • Do you have to pay to attend the event?

    No, the event is totally free of charge. Join us to witness a unique virtual experience.

  • Is this year’s event different from previous years’ events?

    As the event will be held virtually for the 3rd time, the organizing committee has arranged a lot of activities to give the delegates a whole new experience. This year, there will be more interesting and worthwhile sessions that will help you to build a good volunteering journey in IEEE as well. Join us and grab everything on that day.

  • How can I register for the event?

    Registrations are done through our website and are open to any eager individual. Since we take only a limited number of delegates from each Student Branch we encourage everyone interested to register asap.

  • Is this a professional development event?

    IEEE Boost 2022 comprises not only professional development sessions but also activities that enhance your soft skills, and the opportunity to connect with other delegates who have got the same interest as you. It will be the perfect opportunity to kick start your IEEE journey.
